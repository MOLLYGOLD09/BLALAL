Junior High School

Grades 7 to 10 Comprehensive Education Program

Program Overview

The Junior High School (JHS) is designed to provide students in Grades 7 to 10 with a comprehensive and holistic education. It is part of the K-12 educational system implemented to enhance the country's education quality. The Junior High School (JHS) seeks to provide a well-rounded education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for their future academic and career pursuits.
